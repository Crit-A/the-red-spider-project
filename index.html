Wait, what?

The project is like a small, self-contained operating system layered on top of your actual operating system. xkcd provides the background theme, which makes the programs potentially amusing, interesting or touching. People can add programs at will. It's an open-ended process without deadlines or a final goal and we're very open to ideas, programming languages and people. If you like coding and xkcd too, please consider joining the project!

Pointers

In case you're full of questions, we have a Readme and a wiki. If you wonder who we are or want to introduce yourself or your idea, check out our thread at the xkcd forums. We also have an IRC channel: #redspider at foonetic.net. If you want to help out, please have a look at our issues. If you need inspiration, try our list of free ideas.
