Wait, what?

Essentially we're writing a heterogenous set of (small, portable, amusing/interesting/touching, possibly interacting, command-line) programs, which may expand continually. We're not working towards a final goal, just enjoying the process. We're open to ideas, programming languages and people. If you like coding and xkcd too, please consider joining the project!

Pointers

If you're full of questions, you might want to read our wiki and perhaps also our Readme. If you wonder who we are or want to introduce yourself or your idea, check out our thread at the xkcd forums. We also have an IRC channel: #redspider at foonetic.net. If you want to help out, please have a look at our issues.

Artwork

Joey (aka tidbit) made these splendid logo designs. Let us know what you think at the forums! The SVG source is in our repository, but note that you need to have the Segoe Print font installed in order to reproduce the images shown here.
